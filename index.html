    About the Classes

    ---

    Engineering

    Learn hard and soft skills in a colloborative enviroment that looks at desigining functional products through various tools and skillsets including metalworking, woodworking, advanced manufacturing, and programming.

    Computer Science

    Discover the nature of computers and programming by critically solving problems that are relevant to our world by developing apps, websites, software, games and more involving a variety of programming languages.

    Design

    Develop a deeper understanding of design principles and problem solving by applying combinations of mechanical, electrical, and industrial systems to produce real products using an entreprenuerial framework.
